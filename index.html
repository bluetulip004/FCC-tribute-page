Mustafa Kemal Ataturk

A man enlightened millions.

Here are some accomplishments done by him.

  • Made community secular
  • Abolished caliph
  • Abolished parishes
  • Granted women the ability to vote

"Ataturk, with his historic achievements, was one of the greatest leaders of this century (20th). He was a towering figure of human ideals. His extraordinary leadership and his military genius accomplished so much for his country; first in battlefields, and then in building his nation, materially and in spirit. He created a modern and sovereign state from the ashes of a defeated empire. He was a great peace-maker, an ultimate reformer and a torch-bearer of freedom. He won not only the heart of his nation but the hearts of the oppressed nations as well. We all miss him."

--John F. Kennedy
